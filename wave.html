"Touch the Wave"

An 11 by 14 inch mixed media collage that depicts three children captivated by the sight of a monstrous shark forming ripples of waves as it glides through the water. In this piece, I used colorful cutouts from magazines to construct the girls' silhouettes to contrast with the blue monochromatic background that includes both shark and water. During my freshman year, I submitted this piece to the Congressional Art Competition and placed third in the first district of New Jersey.
