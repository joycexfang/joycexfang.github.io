Neighborhood

Project information

  • Category: Painting
  • Award: Congressional Art Competition
  • Project date: June 2018
  • Project URL: Read this Article!

This is an acrylic painting that features two volunteers working to beautify their community. A woman is holding an umbrella for a young girl who is painting a mural of a large yellow peace sign with birds and hearts surrounding it. The idea for my painting was born after walking through the streets of Camden with my family and looking at both graffiti on the walls and areas that are being revitalized. After hours of brainstorming and sketching, I finally developed a piece that showcases the importance of serving oneâ€™s community to create a better place for future generations. I was recognized by U.S. Congressman Donald Norcross as a national winner in the 2018 Congressional Art Competition, in which I represented the 1st District of New Jersey. The Congressional Institute invited me to Washington D.C., where I was honored to have my painting displayed in the Canon Tunnel of the U.S. Capitol for one year.
