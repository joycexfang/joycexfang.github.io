Words of Advice

Never be afraid to ask. My dad once told me, "if you are stuck trying to solve something for hours on end, might as well ask someone for help." This is something I constantly work on as I am learning in college. I ask my professors, mentors, and peers for help whenever I am stuck.

Believe in yourself, even if you think that you aren't capable. With this peace of mind, you are able to achieve so much more. Never stop yourself from reaching your goals.

Start Early. As a professional procrastinator in high school, I found myself stressed all the time. In college, I learned that it's crucial to start early to be successful, especially when you're coding.

Network with people. If you want to work for a company, find someone who already works there. You can ask them for advice and what their experience was like. Being able to know someone who works at your dream company will help you envision yourself there too.
